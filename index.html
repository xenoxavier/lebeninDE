About the Leben in Deutschland Test

Everything you need to know about the German citizenship exam

📋

Test Format

  • 33 multiple-choice questions
  • 60 minutes duration
  • 17 correct answers needed to pass
  • Available in German only
📚

Topics Covered

  • Politics in Democracy
  • History and Responsibility
  • People and Society
  • State-specific questions
💰

Cost & Registration

  • Test fee: €25
  • Register at VHS or test centers
  • Valid ID required
  • Results within 6 weeks
